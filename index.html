Dr. Ritesh.V Sutrave is a highly qualified and experienced neuro-psychiatrist specializing in mental health disorders and de-addiction treatment. With years of dedicated practice, Dr. SUTRAVE provides comprehensive care for patients suffering from various psychiatric conditions and addiction-related issues.

Our Services

OPD Consultation

Outpatient consultation for various psychiatric disorders including depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and other mental health conditions.

IPD Treatment

Inpatient treatment for severe psychiatric conditions requiring intensive care and monitoring in a controlled environment.

M-ECT

Modified Electroconvulsive Therapy for treatment-resistant depression and other severe psychiatric conditions.

Counselling & Psychotherapy

Professional counseling and psychotherapy sessions to help patients cope with mental health challenges and improve their quality of life.

De-Addiction Centre

Comprehensive de-addiction treatment for alcohol, drug, and other substance abuse disorders with personalized care plans.

Family Counseling

Family therapy sessions to help families understand and support their loved ones through mental health recovery.

Sexual Problems Treatment

Comprehensive treatment for sexual health issues including decreased libido, premature ejaculation, and erectile dysfunction with professional care.
