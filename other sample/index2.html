Questions

Things people wonder
before reaching out.

Is everything I say completely confidential?+

Yes. Everything shared in our sessions is protected by law and strict professional ethics. Nothing leaves this space without your written permission — with very rare legal exceptions (imminent danger to self or others).

What if I don't know what to say?+

That's completely okay. Many clients start with "I don't even know where to begin." I'll guide the conversation gently. There are no wrong answers and no pressure to perform. Silence is okay too.

How many sessions will I need?+

This varies for everyone. Some people feel significant shifts in 8–12 sessions; others prefer to continue longer. We check in regularly and let your goals guide the pace. You're never locked in.

Is online therapy really as effective?+

Research shows online therapy is equally effective as in-person for most concerns. Many clients find the comfort of their own environment helps them open up more. We use the same evidence-based approaches.

I've never done therapy before — is that okay?+

Absolutely. Many of my clients are coming to therapy for the first time. We start slowly, build trust, and go at your pace. You'll never feel rushed or judged for being new to this.

Can I stop sessions whenever I want?+

Yes, always. Therapy is entirely your choice. You can pause, take a break, or end sessions at any time. I'll always support whatever decision feels right for you.
